About

I am a second year PhD student in the Warwick Mathematics and Statistics CDT. My interests are, broadly, in regression modelling, online methods and high-dimensional problems. I completed my bachelor's degree in Actuarial Science and Mathematical Statistics at the University of Cape Town, and then a MSc in Statistics at the University of Warwick.
